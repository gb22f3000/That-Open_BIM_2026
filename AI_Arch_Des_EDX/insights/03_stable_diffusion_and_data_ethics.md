## Step 6: Data Structure & Refinery

### Data Structure
Images, text prompts, numerical metrics, geospatial coordinates

### Refinery Steps
1. **Collection** - Web scraping, API calls, manual annotation
2. **Preprocessing** - Cleaning, normalization, transformation
3. **Annotation** - Labeling, tagging, describing
4. **Augmentation** - Image rotation/flipping, text paraphrasing
5. **Filtering** - Remove low-quality or irrelevant data
6. **Formatting** - Convert to JSON/CSV, organize datasets
7. **Quality Check** - Verify accuracy, diversity, representation

---

## Step 7: Ethics of Data

### Privacy Concerns
- **Raw data**: PII in images/text, location tracking, biometric data, IP/copyright
- **Metadata**: User behavior tracking, unclear provenance, inference biases

### Social Impacts
- Cultural/historical sensitivity
- Political/social activism misuse
- Graphic or disturbing content
- Stereotyping and bias perpetuation

### Mitigation Strategies
- Data anonymization and pseudonymization
- Robust data protection policies
- Regular audits and risk assessments
- Transparent provenance documentation
- Fairness and accountability in AI models

### Regulatory Compliance
GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, COPPA
