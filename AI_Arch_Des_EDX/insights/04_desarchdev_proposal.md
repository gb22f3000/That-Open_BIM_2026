# DesArchdev - AI-Powered Architectural Design Tool

## Application Overview

**DesArchdev** leverages AI to generate innovative, sustainable, and functional building designs based on site conditions, climate, and user requirements. It upgrades the **conceptual design phase**.

## Affected Design Areas
1. Conceptual Design
2. Schematic Design
3. Space Planning
4. Massing Studies
5. Building Information Modeling (BIM)
6. Fire & Life Safety regulatory checking

---

## Key Features

### Design Generation
- Building, facade, and space planning design
- Optimized floor plans and circulation paths

### Design Optimization
- Energy efficiency, natural light, structural integrity

### Urban & Interior Design
- City planning, urban design, room and furniture design

### Visualization & Sustainability
- Photorealistic renderings, virtual tours
- Energy analysis, carbon footprint assessment

### Collaborative Design
- Human-AI co-creation
- Multidisciplinary collaboration
- Automated design generation with real-time evaluation
- BIM integration and fire compartmentation evaluation

---

## Step 2: Data & Model

### Input Data
- Site information (text, images, 3D models)
- Design requirements (text)
- Climate data
- Building codes and regulations
- User preferences

### Output Data
- Optimized building designs (2D/3D CAD)
- Design evaluation reports
- Energy efficiency analysis
- Visualizations and renderings

### AI Models
| Model | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| GANs | Design generation |
| GNNs | Building performance optimization |
| NLP | Design requirement analysis |
| CNNs | Image analysis |

**Model Type:** Primarily **generative** with discriminative aspects for performance evaluation.
